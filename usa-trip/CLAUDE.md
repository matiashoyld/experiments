# USA Trip Comparison Tool

Interactive comparison site for choosing between three family trip options (August 2026, from SFO).

## Options compared
1. **Seattle + Pacific Northwest** — city + nature, lowest cost
2. **Yellowstone + Grand Teton** — iconic national park, highest cost
3. **Banff, Canada** — most scenic, free park entry 2026

## Tech stack
- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS — single `index.html`
- Deployed on Vercel

## Local development
Open `index.html` in a browser. No build step.

## Files
- `index.html` — interactive comparison site with tabs, cost breakdowns, day-by-day itineraries
- `*.md` files — detailed research notes per destination and pricing research

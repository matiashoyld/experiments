### Key Takeaways

1. **Canmore is surprisingly competitive on accommodation.** The Canadian dollar discount (1 CAD ~ 0.71 USD) makes Canmore's large rental homes very affordable compared to US destinations. A 5BR/4.5BA townhome sleeping 12 with panoramic mountain views and hot tub runs ~CA $476/night (US $338/night).

2. **West Yellowstone is the most expensive for accommodation.** Peak August demand, limited supply of large homes near the park, and the iconic location drive prices up significantly. Budget $600-800/night for a suitable 6-bedroom property.

3. **Seattle Route offers moderate pricing but requires 4 separate bookings.** Individual segments are cheaper, but the logistical complexity of 4 different properties (with check-in/checkout friction for 12 people) is a real cost.

4. **Supply of 12-person properties is limited everywhere.** For all three destinations, properties sleeping 12+ with 5+ bedrooms represent a tiny fraction of available rentals. Book EARLY -- these fill up fast for August.

5. **Cleaning fees and service fees add 10-20% on top of nightly rates** across all platforms. Factor in an additional $200-500 per booking for cleaning fees plus 10-14% platform service fees.

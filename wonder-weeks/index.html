Luken's Leaps

Leap Chart

🧠 What are leaps?

During his first 20 months, Luken will go through 10 predictable mental developmental leaps. Each leap changes how he perceives the world, unlocking new abilities and understanding.

⛈️ Fussy phases

Before each leap, Luken may become fussy, clingy, and cranky — the "3 C's." His brain is developing rapidly and the world suddenly feels different.

  • Crying — more than usual, harder to soothe
  • Clinginess — wants to be held constantly
  • Crankiness — generally irritable

☀️ Sunny phases

After the fussy phase, Luken has adjusted to his new world. He'll be happy, curious, and eager to practice exciting new abilities!

📅 Why due date matters

All timing is calculated from Luken's due date (Nov 24, 2025), not his birth date. The brain develops on a schedule set from conception.

🩶 The grey period

Around weeks 29–30, there's a fussy phase that isn't a leap. Luken will be processing the concept of distance — realizing someone can walk away. It typically lasts about one week.
